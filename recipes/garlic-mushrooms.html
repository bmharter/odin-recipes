Garlic Mushrooms

A delicious side to any meat dish, mushrooms are both tasty and healthy!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 lbs. sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp. dried basil
  • 1 cup red wine

Directions

  1. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add mushrooms and garlic; cook and stir until mushrooms are a light golden brown and liquid has evaporated, about 10 minutes. Stir in basil.
  2. Reduce heat to low, and pour wine into the skillet. Simmer until wine has mostly evaporated, and a spoon briefly leaves a trail behind it when drug through. Serve immediately.
